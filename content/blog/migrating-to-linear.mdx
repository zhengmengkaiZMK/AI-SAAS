## Why Linear?

Linear has become the go-to project management tool for high-performing software teams. Its speed, simplicity, and powerful features make it an excellent choice for teams looking to streamline their workflow.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Set Up Your Workspace

Create your workspace and invite your team members. Linear's onboarding flow makes this process seamless.

### Step 2: Configure Your Projects

Organize your work into projects and teams. Each team can have its own workflow and priorities.

### Step 3: Import Existing Issues

Linear provides importers for Jira, GitHub, and other popular tools, making migration straightforward.

## Best Practices

<Steps>

### Use Keyboard Shortcuts

Linear is designed for speed. Learn the keyboard shortcuts to navigate and create issues quickly.

### Set Up Automations

Configure workflows to automatically assign issues, update statuses, and notify team members.

### Integrate with Your Tools

Connect Linear with GitHub, Slack, and other tools your team uses daily.

</Steps>

## Conclusion

Linear's focus on speed and simplicity can transform how your team manages software projects. Start small, learn the basics, and gradually adopt more advanced features.

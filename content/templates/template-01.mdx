## Introduction

The Buffett Portfolio Template is designed for value investors, helping you track real-time changes in Warren Buffett's and Berkshire Hathaway's investment portfolio. Through visual charts and data analysis, gain deep insights into the essence of value investing.

### Save Time and Costs

Manually tracking and analyzing top investors' portfolio changes is time-consuming and error-prone. This template provides a ready-to-use solution that automatically updates holdings data and generates analysis reports. Get professional-grade investment analysis tools in minutes without spending thousands on development costs.

### Scalable by Design

Whether you're an individual investor or professional analyst, this template grows with your needs. From basic portfolio tracking to advanced strategy analysis, the modular architecture lets you easily expand functionality without rebuilding from scratch.

### Tailored for Investors

Every detail is designed for investment analysis. Clear data presentation, intuitive charts, and real-time portfolio updates help you make quick investment decisions. Build a professional and credible investment analysis platform.

### Developer-Friendly and Customizable

Built with TypeScript and a type-safe codebase that's easy to maintain and extend. Fully customize the design to match your brand and adapt components for specific needs. Perfect for new projects and redesigns, providing flexibility and reliability for long-term projects.

## What is Money New?

Money New is a cognitive financial community that combines:

- **Stock Dashboard** - Track multiple stocks with real-time data
- **Investment Notes** - Record and review your market analysis
- **Options Learning** - Master options trading from fundamentals to advanced strategies
- **Community** - Share insights and learn from other investors

## Core Philosophy

> "The flow of money reflects the update of human cognition."

Money New believes that better investment decisions come from:

1. **Visualization** - Seeing market data clearly
2. **Documentation** - Recording your thoughts and rationale
3. **Validation** - Testing your predictions against reality
4. **Community** - Learning from collective intelligence

## User Interface Overview

### Navigation Bar

Access key sections:

- **Home** - Your main dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

1. **Market Overview** - Major indices and trends
2. **Your Stocks** - Cards for each tracked stock
3. **Recent Notes** - Your latest analysis

Each stock card displays:

- Quick actions (add note, remove, details)
- News headlines

## Best Practices

### For Stock Tracking

1. Start with 5-10 stocks you understand well
2. Review your dashboard daily
3. Set price alerts for key levels
4. Keep notes updated with new information

### For Note-Taking

1. Write immediately after making decisions
2. Include your reasoning, not just the action
3. Tag notes for easy categorization
4. Review past notes weekly

### For Options Learning

1. Start with the basics
2. Practice paper trading before real money
3. Focus on one strategy at a time
4. Track all trades in your notes

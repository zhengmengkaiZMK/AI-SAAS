## Welcome to Money New

Money New is a cognitive financial community that helps you track investments, take notes, and learn about options trading. This guide will help you get started quickly.

## Create Your Account

1. Visit [Money New](/) and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email address or use Google sign-in
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

## Add Your First Stock

Once you're logged in, you can start tracking stocks:

1. Click the "Add Stock" button in your dashboard
2. Search for a stock by symbol (e.g., AAPL, TSLA, GOOGL)
3. Click "Add to Dashboard" to start tracking

## Explore the Dashboard

Your dashboard provides a real-time overview of:

- **Market trends** - Global market movements at a glance
- **Stock cards** - Visual charts for each stock you're tracking
- **Performance metrics** - Key indicators and price changes
- **News feed** - Latest updates about your tracked stocks

## Take Notes

Record your investment thoughts and analysis:

1. Click on any stock card
2. Select "Add Note"
3. Write your observations, predictions, or strategy
4. Tag notes for easy retrieval later

## Learn Options Trading

Navigate to the **Options Content** section to:

- Understand basic options terminology
- Learn about calls and puts
- Study volatility and risk management
- Practice with examples and case studies
